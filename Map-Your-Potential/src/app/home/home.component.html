Map Your Potential is a career matching platform that helps people find their ideal career in technology. It accomplishes this in two ways. The first is by taking the career assessment test. Through a series of questions, we evaluate your compatibility with various technical roles and predict the top three possible matches. The second is by browsing through the different career profiles which contains numerous informative graphs and statistics about the roles. This is aimed at helping people understand and enter careers. We also have a job posting section which allows you to search for current opportunities in the field of your interest.
